OpenFPS Server Channels

Created:
UDP 6000 - LISTENING / SENDING (Client)
TCP 25565 - LISTENING (Server) ...Server listening tcp channel
UDP 25565 - LISTENING (Server) ...Server listening udp channel
UDP 65240<arbitrary> - SENDING (Server) ...Server client udp channel

On terminate:
-UDP 6000 terminates with an error.
-TCP 25565 terminates successfully.
-UDP 25565 terminates with an error.
-UDP 65240<arbitrary> terminates with an error.

Possible errors:
-TCP channels successfully terminate.
-All UDP channels terminate with an error 
-UDP channels aren't fully disconnecting without WINSOCK DISCONNECT
